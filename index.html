About Maulishree

Maulishree is a passionate 13-year-old with an exceptional love for both Physics and Mathematics. Numbers, equations, and the mysteries of Physics are her playground, where she finds joy in solving complex problems and uncovering new ideas. Whether she's singing Heart Attack or unraveling mathematical theorems, Maulishree brings fun and curiosity to everything she touches.

In addition to her love for the sciences, Maulishree enjoys immersing herself in captivating stories. Her favorite series is the Twisted series by Ana Huang, where characters like Alex Volkov and Rhys Larsen fuel her imagination. The rich world of romance and intricate relationships is something she truly enjoys reading about.
